ROSA.

Herr Strubel lent me a book once. A maid of honor came into that, too. I'm sure it's a trick!


FRAU_LINDEMANN.

[Looking out toward the back.]

Dear,dear, isn't that Herr Strubel now, coming up the hill? To-day of all days! What on earth does he always want up here?

ROSA.

[Pointedly.]

He's in such favor at the Inn. He won't be leaving here all day.
